Each year, the UH Alumni Association pays tribute to the fastest growing Cougar-owned or operated businesses. On February 13, 2023, honorees were recognized at the 9th Annual Cougar 100 Luncheon held at the Bayou City Events Center. During the program, UH alumna, Natara Branch (’98), CEO of Houston Exponential discussed the importance of rising above our challenges to achieve greatness.

9th Annual Cougar 100 Honoree Listing.

Cougar 100 members are automatically included in our Cougar Business Network. The Cougar Business Network is an online listing of all Cougar-owned or operated businesses, which helps promote Cougars doing business with Cougars. Last April, the UH Alumni Association hosted a reception for the Cougar Business Network where over 40 participants gathered to connect and exchange business information.

If you are a Cougar-owned or led business and would like to have your company listed on our alumni platform, CoogsConnect, please join CoogsConnect, and you can control you own listing.
