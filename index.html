Greetings Cougars,

We are pleased to share the UH Alumni Association Annual report with you. This report presents a recap of the engagement activities, events and accomplishments from the 2022 calendar year. The highlights that are shared are the result of our growing alumni community coming together to impact the University of Houston in unique and powerful ways.


As you will see, the opportunities to engage are endless, and I encourage all alumni to explore how they can increase their involvement; participate or volunteer at an event, mentor a fellow Cougar, or serve on a board or committee.
Thank you for being an integral part of our UH family and for helping make 2022 a banner year. Enjoy seeing some great examples of what we have been able to accomplish together. As you know, Cougars always finish strong.

We look forward to crossing paths with you soon.
Sincerely,

Wayne Luckett ('84)

PARTY
BY THE
PLAZA

5,922 Participants

UH alumni came out in droves to partake in the ultimate tailgate experience at Party on the Plaza. Held four hours before each home football game, various Alumni Associations and college groups set up individual spaces at TDECU Stadium for fun and refreshments. Life Members were also invited to their exclusive Life Member tailgate to take part in the excitement.

COOGS
ON THE
ROAD

765 Participants

UH alumni and friends traveled to support the Cougars at various away athletic events in 2022. During March Madness, UH alumni cheered on the Cougars’ Men’s Hoops as they advanced to the Elite 8 in the NCAA tournament after defeating No. 1 seed Arizona. Hundreds of alumni traveled to San Antonio as the Cougars’ Men’s Hoops faced off against Villanova.

Invitational Golf tournament

On June 16, 2022, the UH Alumni Association held its annual Invitational Golf Tournament at the Sienna Golf Club in Missouri City, Texas. With 120 participants and a little friendly competition, winning team members included Chuck Arnold (’94), Colby Arnold, Darian Ruiz and Will Sammons.

Community
Presence

Cougar spirit spills out of main campus and into the streets of the entire Houston region. The official Spirit of Houston marching band, cheerleaders and alumni participate in various parades each year.

Alumni Oral
History
Project

The UH Alumni Association partnered with PCI (Publishing Concepts, Inc.) to produce an Oral History publication—a collection of stories from UH alumni throughout the years. The 100th Anniversary of the University of Houston is on the horizon in 2027, so it is important to preserve these treasured memories for generations to come. We thank our alumni for providing a glimpse into their UH experiences and know that their dedication to this special endeavor will be appreciated by those that follow.

Affiliated Alumni Associations and Networks

42 Affiliated
Alumni
Associations

Affiliated Alumni Associations are made up of Cougars who share a common bond. These volunteer-led groups create a way for UH alumni to connect based on the college that they attended or shared interest. Each year, these groups provide scholarship support to deserving students as well as participate in various community service-related activities.
